Lasagna

Description

this is one of the most famous and wanted dishes all around the world

ingredients :

  • Spagetti
  • Tomato
  • Garlic
  • Parsley
  • Meat
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the Spagetti
  2. Put the Tomato Garlic and Parsley in blender
  3. pour the sauce with spagettin in a pan
  4. stir and add meeat
  5. finally spray some cheese when serving
